About Us

GenPur (which means trust in farming)CBO is a registered Community-Based Organization located in Northern Uganda. Its was founded in 2024, to responds to the needs of vulnerable children, particularly those from post-conflict communities. Our mission is to empower communities by fostering participation in social and economic development activities, aimed at improving the quality of life in underserved areas of Uganda. We strive to create programs that inspire collaboration, resilience, and upward mobility for all.

GENPUR COMMUNITY BASED ORGANIZATION PROGRAMS

Uplifting from communities through 4 programs.

Through community, stakeholders involvement and system strengthening, GenPur in partnership with other stakeholders focuses on the following program areas

Child Education Support

We provide access to quality education for vulnerable children: Focusing on school enrollment, learning materials, partial school fees support and mentorship. 

Read more

Household Strengthening & Wellbeing

We create safe, nurturing homes where children can thrive through: Supporting families to build resilience and stability. Promote health, nutrition, parenting skills, and emotional wellbeing.

Read more

Livelihood & Economic Empowerment

We enable families to achieve self-reliance and sustainable income through:   Equiping families with practical skills and financial literacy.   Providing training on Income generating activities and the saving initiatives including village saving loaning association

Read more

Sports / Coaching Boys into Men

We guide boys to grow into responsible, compassionate men who uplift their communities. Use sports as a tool for mentorship and character building.    Teach discipline, teamwork, respect, and positive masculinity.
