About Us

GenPur (which means trust in farming)CBO is a registered Community-Based Organization located in Northern Uganda. Its was founded in 2024, to responds to the needs of vulnerable children, particularly those from post-conflict communities. Our mission is to empower communities by fostering participation in social and economic development activities, aimed at improving the quality of life in underserved areas of Uganda. We strive to create programs that inspire collaboration, resilience, and upward mobility for all.
