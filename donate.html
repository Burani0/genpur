About GenPur Community Based Organization

One Donation. A Lifetime of Impact.

Opportunity is not equal until now. GenPur CBO is dedicated to breaking the cycle of poverty in Amuru District, Northern Uganda, through comprehensive community development programs that transform lives.

Your donation supports our four core programs: Child Education Support, Household Strengthening and Wellbeing, Livelihood and Economic Empowerment, and Sports/Coaching Boys into Men. Together, these programs address the root causes of poverty and create sustainable pathways to prosperity.

Your contribution does more than provide immediate assistance. It breaks cycles of poverty, builds economic agency, and uplifts entire families for generations to come.
